This post shows how to add bibliography references to simple blog posts without relying on Jekyll-specific citation tags. For example, you can write simple citations such as Einstein (1950), multiple citations such as Einstein (1950) and Einstein (1905), or longer inline references in regular Markdown.

> Einstein (1905), "On the Electrodynamics of Moving Bodies."
